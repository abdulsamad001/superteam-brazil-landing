FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Superteam Brazil? ‚ñº
Superteam Brazil is the Brazilian branch of Superteam, a global network of talented builders in the Solana ecosystem. We connect developers, designers, creators, and founders with opportunities, resources, and a thriving community to build the future of Web3 in Brazil.
How do I become a member? ‚ñº
Join our Telegram or Discord community and introduce yourself! We welcome builders of all skill levels. Attend our events, participate in discussions, and start contributing to the community. Active members can apply to become official Superteam Brazil members.
What opportunities are available? ‚ñº
We offer bounties, grants, full-time job opportunities, freelance projects, hackathons, and mentorship programs. Members get exclusive access to opportunities from leading Solana projects and ecosystem partners.
How can projects partner with us? ‚ñº
If you're a Solana project looking to connect with talented Brazilian builders, reach out to us via Telegram or email. We can help with talent sourcing, community building, event sponsorship, and ecosystem development in Brazil.
Do I need to be a developer to join? ‚ñº
Not at all! We welcome designers, content creators, community managers, marketers, business developers, and anyone passionate about Web3 and Solana. There are opportunities for all types of builders in our community.
